# Customer Feedback Notes

## Synthetic customer need

The simulated startup needs an assistant that reduces routine support load without creating commercial, legal, or customer-success risk.

## Deployment feedback

- Routine questions should stay fast and low-cost.
- Finance, legal, compliance, and high-value churn-risk cases need a human-review gate.
- Policy-sensitive answers need grounded citations or escalation.
- Tool-routing failures should be visible before rollout, not discovered through customer complaints.

## Product feedback

Expose review thresholds and failure-mode summaries in the operator surface. Treat hallucinated policy and missed escalation as launch blockers.
